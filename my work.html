alliance high school

this is a school located in kiambu county.The school was founded in the year 1926 by a group of churches.The school was mainly built to cater for the africans who wanted to quench there thirst for education.The first principal of this great institution was the great Edward carey Francis who was appointed by the colonialists to head the school.
